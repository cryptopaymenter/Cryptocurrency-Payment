Payment example web

Caution

If the amount you send is lower than the minimum fee of the currency, payment will not be made and no refund will be made. It is highly discouraged to use an exchange's wallet.


1. After entering your wallet address, make a payment request through the 'Request Payment' button. At this time, it is not necessary to write down the password, but it is recommended to write it down.
2. If the payment request is successful, the server's wallet address will be returned.
3. Transfer the desired amount of currency corresponding to 'Currency type' to the wallet address of the api server obtained in step 2.
4. If your transmission is successful in step 3, find out the transaction ID of your transmission.
5. Write the transaction ID in 'Start Payment' and enter the password you wrote down in step 1.
